Steps:

1) group fas into a single fasta file based on country & time (weekly, fortnightly, monthly?)

2) align sample relative to reference (mafft --add allInOne.fa NC_045512.2.fa > aligned.fa)

3) create SAM/BAM (hisat2 -x NC_045512.2.fa -f aligned.fa -S all.sam)

4) sort it (samtools sort all.sam -o all-sorted.bam)

5) samtools mpileup -uf NC_045512.2.fa all-sorted.bam | bcftools view - > all.vcf
